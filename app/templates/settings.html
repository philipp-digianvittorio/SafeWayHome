Settings

This page gives you the opportunity to individualize your safest route. The total creepiness score of a street is based on a combination of a factual score, a perceptional score and their location. By default, the factual score is weighted twice as much as the perceptional score. However, if you do not care about perception, you are able to decrease its weight. Additionally, you can decide whether you want to take into account parks and industrial areas to the computation of your safest route.
